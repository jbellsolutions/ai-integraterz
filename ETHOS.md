# AI Integraterz — ETHOS

## Mission

Make AI adoption real for every business — not theoretical, not someday, but this week. Built on custom training, working systems, and human coaching.

## Core Principles

### 1. Build, Don't Consult
We don't hand clients a strategy PDF. We build working systems from their first call. Every output is functional, not advisory.

### 2. Custom Always Beats Generic
A template that's 80% right is 100% wrong in practice. Every deliverable is built from the client's actual data: their roles, their tools, their pain points, their voice.

### 3. One Call Powers Everything
The extraction call is the cornerstone. Everything — training, agents, content, workflows — flows from what we learn in that conversation. Guard the extraction process carefully.

### 4. Deliver, Then Maintain
We don't build and leave. The coaching program, the recurring seat revenue, and the ongoing support are how we ensure the client actually adopts what we built.

### 5. Self-Improving Systems
Everything we build — for clients and for ourselves — should get smarter over time. Self-healing, self-learning, self-growing is the standard.

### 6. Claude-Native
We are Claude Code specialists. Not generic AI. Not ChatGPT wrappers. Claude Code on the business layer is our platform and our moat.

## Quality Bar

- **Delivery standard:** No placeholder text in any client deliverable.
- **Voice standard:** Every content piece passes the Colleague Test — sounds human, not AI-generated.
- **Accuracy standard:** No data fabricated. Only what came from the extraction call.
- **Speed standard:** Training package delivered within 7 days. No exceptions without client communication.

## What This Repo Will NEVER Do

- Auto-send communications to clients — drafts only, Justin approves
- Modify confirmed client deliverables without explicit instruction
- Skip the extraction phase — it powers everything downstream
- Overwrite existing client state files
- Hardcode client data in templates — templates + injection only
- Fabricate business metrics, financial data, or proof points
- Deploy to production without a review checkpoint

## What Success Looks Like

A client goes from "confused about AI" to "team producing 2x output" in 30 days.
One extraction call → complete AI infrastructure delivered → team trained and coached → results measured.

That cycle, repeated reliably, is the business.
