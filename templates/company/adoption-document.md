## Our AI Philosophy

1. **AI assists, humans decide.** AI handles research, drafts, summaries, and analysis. Humans make the final call.
2. **Specific beats generic.** Use AI for YOUR actual work, not hypothetical examples.
3. **Document what works.** When you find a prompt or workflow that saves you time, share it.
4. **Accuracy first.** Always review AI output before using it. Especially for anything going to clients.

## Team Rollout Plan

### Phase 1 (Week 1-2): Leadership
- {{OWNER_NAME}} sets up Claude Code and completes personal game plan
- Review this adoption document with the full team
- Identify the "AI champion" on each team (the person who'll lead adoption)

### Phase 2 (Week 2-3): Managers and Team Leads
- Each manager completes their role-specific training track
- First coaching session: discuss what's working
- Set baseline metrics (how long does [key task] take today?)

### Phase 3 (Week 3-4): Full Team
- All team members complete their role-specific training
- First full-team AI check-in: what's working, what's confusing?
- Start tracking time saved per person

### Phase 4 (Week 4+): Optimization
- Monthly AI review meeting (first Monday of each month)
- Update this document with new workflows that work
- Measure ROI: time saved × hourly rate = monthly value

## What Requires Human Review

**Always review before using:**
- Anything going to a client (emails, proposals, reports)
- Any financial figures or data
- Legal or compliance language
- Anything with a customer's personal information

**OK to use directly:**
- Internal notes and meeting summaries
- Draft outlines for you to fill in
- Research and information gathering
- First drafts of internal communications
