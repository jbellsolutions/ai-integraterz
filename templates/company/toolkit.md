## Tool Policies

**What to put into AI tools:**
- Internal business documents and processes
- Your own writing (for editing and improvement)
- Public information for research
- Your own data and metrics

**What NOT to put into AI tools:**
- Client personal information without consent
- Passwords, API keys, or credentials
- Confidential legal documents (without legal team approval)
- Proprietary competitor information
