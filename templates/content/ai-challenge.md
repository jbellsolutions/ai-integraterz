## The Challenge

30 days. One AI task per day. Track your wins. Top performer wins {{CHALLENGE_PRIZE}}.

This isn't a test. It's a competition to see who gets the most out of AI in the next month. The winner isn't the person who knows the most about AI — it's the person who uses it the most on real work.

**Scoring:**
- Complete daily prompt: 1 point
- Share your win in team channel: 2 points
- Teach a teammate a workflow you discovered: 5 points
- Build a new SOP (not in your library): 10 points
- Get a reaction of "that's impressive" from {{OWNER_NAME}}: 20 points

**Leaderboard updated:** Every Friday in {{CHALLENGE_TRACKER_LOCATION}}

## WEEK 1: TRY IT (Days 1-7)
*Goal: Make AI feel normal. One small experiment per day.*

| Day | Challenge | Prompt to Use |
|-----|-----------|---------------|
| 1 | Use Claude to write the first draft of something you'd normally write yourself | "Write a first draft of [thing]. Here's what I need it to say: [context]." |
| 2 | Use Claude to summarize something long | "Summarize this in 5 bullet points. The key things I need are: [what matters]. [paste content]" |
| 3 | Ask Claude to give you feedback on your own work | "Review this [document/email/plan] and tell me what's weak and what to improve." |
| 4 | Use Claude to prep for a meeting | "I have a meeting about [topic] with [who]. Help me prepare 5 sharp questions to ask." |
| 5 | Ask Claude something you've been wondering about your industry | "I work in [industry] and I've been wondering: [question]. Give me your honest take." |
| 6 | Use Claude to respond to a difficult email or message | "Help me respond to this in a way that's [tone]: [paste the message]." |
| 7 | WEEK 1 SHARE: Post your best win from the week | "Help me write one paragraph about the most useful thing AI did for me this week." |
